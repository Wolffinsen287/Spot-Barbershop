# Images Folder

Place your images here for the website.

## Hero Background Image

**File name:** `hero-bg.jpg` (or `hero-bg.png`)
- This will be the main background image on your homepage
- **Recommended size:** 1920x1080 pixels or larger
- **Format:** JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Tips:** 
  - Use a high-quality image that represents your barbershop
  - The image will have a dark overlay to ensure text is readable
  - Horizontal/landscape orientation works best

## Gallery Images

For your haircut gallery section, add images with descriptive names like:
- `cut-1.jpg`
- `cut-2.jpg`
- `fade-style.jpg`
- `beard-trim.jpg`
- etc.

**Recommended specs:**
- **Size:** 800x800 pixels (square) or 1200x800 pixels
- **Format:** JPG or WebP for best web performance
- **Optimization:** Compress images before uploading to ensure fast loading times

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Optimize images:** Use tools like TinyPNG or Squoosh to compress images
2. **Use descriptive names:** Makes it easier to manage multiple images
3. **Consistent quality:** Try to maintain similar lighting and quality across all photos
4. **File size:** Keep individual images under 500KB for fast loading
